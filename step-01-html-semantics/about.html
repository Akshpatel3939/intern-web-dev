About Me

My Story

Hi! I'm Alex Johnson, a third-year Computer Science student at Riverside University. I've always been drawn to the intersection of technology and creativity — and web development is exactly where those two worlds meet. Whether it's crafting a pixel-perfect layout or debugging a tricky JavaScript function, I love every part of the process.

This year, I landed my first internship as a Web Developer Intern, and it has been a transformative experience. I've had the chance to work on real products used by real people, which has given me a deep appreciation for writing code that is not only functional but also inclusive and accessible. I strongly believe that the web should work for everyone — regardless of disability, device type, or internet speed.

Outside of coding, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, reading about UX research, and helping friends build their first websites. I'm actively learning React and Node.js to expand my skill set into full-stack development. My goal is to join a team that cares about building things that matter — and to keep growing every single day.
